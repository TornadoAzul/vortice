## How to Use Areas

**Examples of what to put here:**

1. **Career**
   - Professional goals
   - Skills to develop
   - Certifications or courses
   - Networking

2. **Finance**
   - Monthly budget
   - Financial goals
   - Investments
   - Savings

3. **Health**
   - Exercise routine
   - Eating habits
   - Medical checkups
   - Wellness goals

4. **Learning**
   - Topics studying
   - Books to read
   - Courses in progress
   - New skills

---

## Area Review

**Suggested Frequency:** Monthly or Quarterly

Ask yourself:
- Is this area getting enough attention?
- Is there something I need to change or improve?
- What projects could help this area?
